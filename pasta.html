College Kid's Pasta

Pasta

This recipe is something I came up with when I did not have much left in my kitchen Sophomore year of College.

I found it incredibly easy to make and I highly encourage it to be made with the Garlic Butter Steak recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Acquire 10 Servings of Fettuccine
  • 1 Quart of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Cup of Low Moisture Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup of Chili Garlic Paste
  • 1 Chicken Bouillon Cube
  • 2 Packets of Your Choice of Goya Seasoning
  • 4 Tablespoons of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Stick of Garlic Herb Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cento's Tomato Paste
  • 2-10 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 White or Yellow Onion
  • 4 Cups of Portobello Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Tablespoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tablespoon of Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Cumin
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of Pink Himalayan Salt
  • 1-2 Choices of Meat and/or Veggie (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Bring roughly 8 cups of water to a boil with some oil and salt water generously
  2. Place fettuccine into the rapidly boiling water for six minutes or until half cooked
  3. Once fettuccine is done, set aside two cups of pasta water and strain the fettuccine
  4. In a separate pot add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to brown your garlic, mushroom, and onion
  5. Add your tomato paste
  6. Pour your quart of heavy whipping cream
  7. When the heavy cream simmers add half a cup of parmesan cheese
  8. Add your powdered spices
  9. Stir occasionally to prevent burning
  10. Once your sauce begins to thicken add one cup of pasta water
  11. After stirring thoroughly add the fettuccine and cook as desired
  12. Stir in the rest of the cheese and add your butter
  13. Salt to taste and serve
